INTRODUCTION

Artificial Intelligence (Al) is becoming a major part of healthcare. It helps doctors diagnose diseases, manage data, and improve patient care. However, must be considered to keep patients safe.

Ethical Concerns
  1. Privacy and Security: Protecting patient medical information.
  2. Bias and Fairness: Ensuring Al does not discriminate.
  3. Transparency: Explaining how Al systems make decisions.
  4. Accountability: Knowing who is responsible for Al mistakes.
  5. Human Oversight: Doctors should always make final decisions.

Benefits of Al in Healthcare
  • Faster diagnosis and treatment
  • Reduced human error
  • Better patient monitoring
  • More efficient hospitals

Al systems can process huge amounts of medical data in seconds. This helps doctors make better and faster decisions for patients.
